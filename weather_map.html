    Wilmarie's Weather Watch

    Originally founded in 1972 in Dallas, Texas. WWW was first started because the owner Wilmarie was tired of being dressed inappropriately for the weather due to lack of weather information. She soon decided she would create her own place for unbiased weather information that helped anemic people start their day correctly by knowing the weather. It was all a dream given to her by God to put into motion, and through hard work and dedication, it came true.
